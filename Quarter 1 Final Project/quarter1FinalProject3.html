The Classic Example

Taken from this source, the astronomical scale of 52! is often demonstrated using this class example--

  • Stand on the equator, and take one step every billion years.
  • Each time you complete one full circumference, take one drop of water from the Pacific Ocean.
  • When the ocean is empty, take one sheet of paper and place it at sea level.
  • Refill the ocean, and repeat steps 1-3 until the stack of paper reaches from the Earth to the Sun.
  • Reset everything, and complete the process 1000 times.

The number of seconds that have passed since the beginning are only around ONE THIRD of 52!!
