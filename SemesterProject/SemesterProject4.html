For a room with 10 people in it, there is an 11.69% chance that at least two people have the same birthday.

For a room with 25 people in it, there is a 56.87% chance that at least two people have the same birthday.

For a room with 50 people in it, there is a 97% chance that at least two people have the same birthday.

For a room with 100 people in it, there is a 99.99999% chance that at least two people have the same birthday.


Dice Probabilities (not to be used for gambling or anything...)

  • When rolling two dice, the probability for a sum of 1 is 0.
  • Sum of 2 ---> 1/36
  • Sum of 3 ---> 1/18
  • Sum of 4 ---> 1/12
  • Sum of 5 ---> 1/9
  • Sum of 6 ---> 5/36
  • Sum of 7 ---> 1/6
  • Sum of 8 ---> 5/36
  • Sum of 9 ---> 1/9
  • Sum of 10 ---> 1/12
  • Sum of 11 ---> 1/18
  • Sum of 12 ---> 1/36
